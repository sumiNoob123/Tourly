BILASPUR

ABOUT DISTRICT

Bilaspur was the capital of a state of the same name

founded in the 7th century also known as Kahlur.

The ruling dynasty were Chandel Rajputs,

who claimed descent from the rulers of Chanderi in present-day Madhya Pradesh.

The town of Bilaspur was founded in 1663.

The state later became a princely state of British India,

and was under the authority of the British province of Punjab.
