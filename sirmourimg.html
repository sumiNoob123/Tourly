SIRMOUR

ABOUT DISTRICT

District Sirmaur is located in outer Himalayas which is commonly known as Shivalik range.

This district is bounded by district Shimla in North Uttrakhand in East, Haryana in South and Distt.

Solan in North-West. Like other parts of Himachal Pradesh

it has beautiful landscapes, bracing climate

big and small game and legendry temples which hold abiding attraction for the tourists.

The river Giri is the biggest river in the district which originates

from Kotkhai/Jubbal Tehsil of Shimla district and flows down in the south-east direction.
