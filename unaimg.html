UNA

ABOUT DISTRICT

Una is a district in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh.

Una shares its border with the Hoshiarpur and Rupnagar districts of Punjab and Kangra,

Hamirpur and Bilaspur district of Himachal Pradesh.

The terrain is generally plain with semi hills.

Una has five tehsils, namely Ghanari, Haroli, Amb, Bangana and Una itself.

It was a tehsil of Hoshiarpur district until the

Punjab Reorganisation Act, 1966 and Kangra district until 1972,

after which it became a district of Himachal Pradesh.
