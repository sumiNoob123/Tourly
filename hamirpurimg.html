HAMIRPUR

ABOUT DISTRICT

Hamirpur district is a part of Chitrakoot Dham Division of Uttar Pradesh state of India.

Hamirpur town is the district headquarters.

It consists of Four Tehsil namely

Hamirpur, Maudaha, Rath,Sarila and Seven Blocks namely Gohand, Kurara, Maudaha, Muskara, Rath, Sarila, Sumerpur.
