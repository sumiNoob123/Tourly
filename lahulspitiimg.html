ABOUT DISTRICT

The Lahaul and Spiti district in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh

consists of the two formerly separate districts of Lahaul and Spiti.

The present administrative centre is Kyelang in Lahaul.

Before the two districts were merged, Kardang was the capital of Lahaul, and Dhankar the capital of Spiti.
